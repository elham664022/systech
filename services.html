Our Services

Comprehensive support for your German education journey

Career Counseling & University Selection

Expert guidance in choosing the right course and university based on your academic background, interests, and career goals.

  • Personalized career assessment
  • University shortlisting based on your profile
  • Course selection guidance
  • Admission requirements analysis

APS Certification Assistance

Complete support for APS certification process, which is mandatory for Indian students applying to German universities.

  • Document preparation guidance
  • Application form filling assistance
  • Interview preparation
  • Follow-up with APS office

Application Support

Comprehensive assistance with university applications, including SOPs, LORs, and CV preparation.

  • Statement of Purpose (SOP) writing
  • Letter of Recommendation (LOR) guidance
  • CV/Resume preparation
  • Application form filling

Visa Filing & Mock Interviews

Expert guidance for student visa application process, including document preparation and interview preparation.

  • Document checklist preparation
  • Application form filling
  • Mock visa interviews
  • Interview preparation guidance

German Language Coaching

Comprehensive German language training from A1 to B2 level, preparing you for both academic requirements and daily life.

  • Structured language courses
  • Test preparation (TestDaF, DSH)
  • Speaking practice sessions
  • Cultural orientation

Blocked Account & Health Insurance

Assistance with financial requirements for studying in Germany, including setting up a blocked account and health insurance.

  • Blocked account setup guidance
  • Health insurance arrangement
  • Financial planning assistance
  • Document preparation

Accommodation & Part-Time Jobs

Support in finding suitable accommodation in Germany and guidance on part-time job opportunities.

  • Accommodation search assistance
  • Housing application support
  • Part-time job guidance
  • Work permit information

Pre-Departure Orientation

Comprehensive pre-departure guidance to prepare you for life in Germany, including cultural orientation and packing tips.

  • Cultural orientation sessions
  • Packing checklist
  • Travel arrangements guidance
  • Student life information
